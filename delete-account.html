Delete Your AppVerb Account

You can delete your AppVerb account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the application. This page describes how to do that and what gets removed.

How to delete your account from within the app

This is the recommended way to delete your account.

  1. Open the AppVerb application on your device
  2. Sign in with the account you wish to delete, if you are signed out
  3. Open the sidebar menu and tap your profile avatar
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Read the warning and confirm that you understand the action is permanent
  6. You will be asked to sign in again to confirm your identity
  7. Your account will be deleted immediately and you will be returned to the sign-in screen

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account, including the email address, display name, and authentication tokens stored by Firebase
  • For users who signed in with Apple: the Apple ID linkage to AppVerb is revoked. After deletion, AppVerb will no longer appear in your iOS Settings under Apple ID → Sign in with Apple → Apps Using Apple ID
  • All locally stored preferences on your device, including language selection, verb lists, sorting preferences, completed tutorials, and ad consent state
  • All educational content downloaded by the application to your device, including audio files (MP3), images, and JSON data files

What is retained

  • Anonymous and aggregated diagnostic data such as crash reports and usage metrics collected by third-party services (for example Firebase Crashlytics or Google AdMob) is not tied to a specific user account and cannot be removed retroactively
  • Information that we are required to retain in order to comply with applicable laws

Timing

Account deletion is immediate. There is no waiting period, no recovery window, and the action cannot be undone. Once deleted, you would need to create a new account to use AppVerb again.

If you cannot access the app

If you have lost access to your device, cannot sign in, or are otherwise unable to complete the in-app deletion, you may request manual deletion by contacting us at the email address below. Please include the email address of the account you wish to delete in your request. Manual deletion requests are processed within 7 days of receipt.
