Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AppVerb ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use the application, certain information may be collected automatically or through third-party services.

Account Information

You may sign in using Firebase Authentication with email/password or Google Sign-in. When you sign in, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication token for the purpose of managing your account. If you sign in with Google, we request access to your profile and email address through Google OAuth 2.0.

Usage Data

The application may collect anonymous usage data such as device type, app interactions, and crash reports to improve the application.

User Preferences

The application stores your preferences locally on your device (such as language selection, verb lists, sorting preferences, and completed tutorials). This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers to provide ads.

When you first use the app, you will be asked whether you consent to personalized advertisements. You can change this preference at any time within the app settings. If you do not consent, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

Device Storage

The application downloads and stores educational content on your device, including audio files (MP3), data files (JSON), and images. These files are stored in the application's private storage area and do not contain personal user data. This allows the app to function offline.

Device Wake Lock

The application may prevent your device from sleeping while audio content is being played. This is released automatically when playback stops.

Subscriptions

In the future, the application may offer optional paid subscriptions. Payments and billing information are processed by the app store provider (such as Google Play) and are not stored by us.

Data Storage and Delivery

Application content such as audio files, images, and data files may be delivered through Cloudflare infrastructure. These files do not contain personal user data. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

Third-Party Services

The application uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication — for user account management
  • Google Sign-in — for authentication via Google account
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements
  • Cloudflare — for content delivery

Data Retention and Deletion

Your account information is retained as long as your account is active. Locally stored preferences and downloaded content can be cleared by uninstalling the application or clearing app data through your device settings. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

The application is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
